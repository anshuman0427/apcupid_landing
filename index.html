Have a question? Check out the FAQ

We’ve answered some of the most common questions founders ask us before getting started. Still curious? We’re just a message away!

🚀 What exactly is apcupidONE?

apcupidONE is a startup studio built by founders—for founders. We help you validate, design, build, and launch your MVP without the usual chaos.

💰 Do I need funding to start working with you?

Nope! We work with bootstrapped founders all the time. We focus on lean experiments and clarity—not bloated budgets.

👨‍💻 Do I need to know how to code?

Not at all. We’ve built a no-fluff process that’s perfect for non-technical founders. You bring the vision, we’ll handle the tech.

🧠 What if I don’t have a co-founder yet?

No worries—many founders start solo. We help you get traction, and if needed, we’ll guide you on finding the right partner later.

⏳ How long does it take to build my MVP?

Depending on scope, most MVPs are ready in 3–12 weeks. We move fast, and we don’t believe in dragging timelines

📞 Can I talk to someone before I commit?

Of course! Just book a free discovery call with our founder team. We love helping people get clarity—even if you don’t work with us.
